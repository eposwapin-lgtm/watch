Why Sterling Row

Affordable luxury, uncompromising style.

Luxury watches used to mean inaccessible prices and pretentious boutiques. We reimagined the experience so you can browse, order, and receive a stunning timepiece in 3–5 days — without breaking the bank.

Premium Japanese quartz movement. Miyota and Seiko calibers power every Sterling Row watch — accurate to ±20 seconds per month, with a 3-year battery life and water resistance up to 30 meters.

Sapphire-coated mineral glass. Scratch-resistant crystal protects every dial. Cases are cast from 316L surgical-grade stainless steel — the same alloy trusted by luxury Swiss houses.

3–5 day delivery. Order by 2 PM CST for same-day dispatch. Every watch arrives in a gift-ready leatherette box with a polishing cloth and adjustable link removal tool.

Hypoallergenic and nickel-free. All straps and clasps are rigorously tested. Rose gold plating uses ion-bonding technology for lasting color that will not fade or tarnish.

Our Atelier

A design studio on Michigan Avenue.

Sterling Row started in 2019 when two watch enthusiasts — James Holt and Priya Shah — left the corporate jewelry world to make beautiful timepieces accessible. They opened a 2,800 sq ft design atelier on Michigan Avenue with one rule: every watch must look like it costs ten times its price.

Six years and 42,000 watches later, that rule still holds. Every chronograph, minimalist dial, and mesh strap is inspected, regulated, and registered so we can service it for life — no matter where you bought it.
